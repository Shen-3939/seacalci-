Applications

Poultry Feed & Nutrition

Natural marine calcium supports strong egg shell formation and healthy bone development in poultry. Suitable for layer and breeder birds requiring consistent calcium intake.

Agriculture & Soil Conditioning

Supplies calcium that supports soil structure, nutrient balance, and long-term soil health. Suitable for sustainable and organic agricultural practices.

Feed Manufacturing

Marine-derived calcium suitable for inclusion in commercial feed formulations. Offers a natural alternative to limestone and synthetic calcium sources.

Ornamental & Pet Birds

Provides a natural calcium supplement that supports beak conditioning and overall bird health. Cleaned and graded for safe and reliable daily use.

Industrial & Processing Applications

Carefully processed calcium material suitable for further grading and value-added applications. Consistent quality supports controlled industrial usage.

Aquaculture & Hatchery

Marine-derived calcium suitable for aquaculture feed formulations and hatchery applications. Designed to support mineral balance and skeletal development in controlled environments.
