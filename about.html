Asixa Marine

Premium Natural Marine Calcium

Asixa Marine is a marine products sourcing and exporting company with a strong foundation in international trade. Since 2015, the company has been supplying high-quality marine-sourced materials to both domestic and international markets, backed by extensive hands-on experience in marine sourcing, supply chain management, and global logistics. Built on long-term sourcing relationships and careful processing practices, Asixa focuses on consistency, quality control, and responsible handling to ensure dependable supply across all markets served.

At every stage—from sourcing and handling to grading and packaging—Asixa emphasizes traceability and strict quality standards. By working closely with trusted suppliers and maintaining transparent business practices, the company ensures products meet the expectations of feed manufacturers, traders, and end-use industries. Driven by evolving market needs, Asixa continues to expand its product portfolio and value-added offerings while remaining committed to reliability, integrity, and long-term partnerships.

SeaCalci

A Brand by Asixa

SeaCalci is a dedicated marine calcium brand by Asixa Marine, developed to deliver naturally sourced calcium products derived from premium cuttlefish bone. Built on Asixa Marine’s strong foundation in marine sourcing and international trade, SeaCalci represents a focused move toward value-added, application-specific marine calcium solutions. Rooted in responsible sourcing practices, all products are carefully cleaned, sun-dried, graded, and processed to ensure natural purity, consistency, and suitability for poultry nutrition, ornamental birds, and agricultural applications.

With a strong emphasis on quality assurance and reliable supply, SeaCalci serves both retail and bulk markets, offering consumer-ready packaging as well as large-volume supply for feed manufacturers and distributors. Backed by years of export experience and supply chain expertise, the brand combines traditional marine knowledge with modern market expectations, standing for transparency, dependable partnerships, and a long-term commitment to delivering natural marine calcium products customers can trust.
