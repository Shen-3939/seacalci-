About Us

Premium Calcium from Cuttlefish Bone

Asixa Marine is a marine products sourcing and exporting company with a strong foundation in international trade. Since 2015, the company has been supplying high-quality marine-sourced materials to domestic and international markets, supported by consistent quality control and reliable supply chain practices.

Building on this experience, SeaCalci was developed as a dedicated brand by Asixa Marine to offer natural marine calcium products derived from premium cuttlefish bone. Combining responsible sourcing with careful processing and grading, SeaCalci delivers dependable calcium solutions for feed, pet, aquaculture, and agricultural applications.
